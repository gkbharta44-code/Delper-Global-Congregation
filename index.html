Methodology & sources

Population & median age: Worldometer, elaboration of UN DESA Population Division, World Population Prospects 2024 Revision (medium-fertility variant), accessed 20 Jul 2026.

Est. % age 18–75: no single public dataset gives the exact 18–75 band for every country, so it is approximated from each country's median age — 50% for the youngest-population countries (median age ≈16) rising to 80% for the oldest (median age ≈50+), interpolated linearly. This is an estimate, not a census figure; substitute official national statistics for precision.

Local members = floor(Members ÷ 1,500)  |  Guild members = floor(Local members ÷ 150)  |  Group members = floor(Guild members ÷ 700). All tiers are floored to whole seats; countries below a tier's threshold show 0.
